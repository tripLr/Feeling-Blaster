FEELING BLASTER ∞

Door ∞ is open.

When the red light bleeds through the wall at 3 a.m.—heart pounding, RSD spiking, that knife twisting in your chest—walk through.

No therapy-speak. No 12-week program. Just six raw truths and a button that gets smaller every time you name it.

Why this exists

At 57, after rage blackouts, marriage ultimatums, and too many nights staring at the ceiling asking “why am I still here,” one thing became clear:

The pain isn’t the enemy. The button is.

That invisible trigger wired in childhood—lit up by every silence, tone shift, or perceived rejection—that screams “you’re worthless” in half a second.

We built this so you can spot the button before it detonates.
Every honest pick shrinks it. Every share saves a brother.

Pick the knife in your chest right now:

1. She’s done with me
2. I just raged and hate myself
3. I’m a worthless failure
4. I want to die
5. I’m trapped, no moves left
6. Someone just humiliated me
